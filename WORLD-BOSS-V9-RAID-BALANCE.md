# World Boss V9 — Raid Balance

- Fixed battle image jumping: one message edit per refresh.
- Boss HP scales from card HP at 4 players up to a capped multiplier for larger raids.
- Faster rage, lower boss miss chance, more frequent summons and curses.
- Player summons now expire by owner turns.
- Physical/magical/holy damage and boss/minion resistances.
- Priest deals bonus holy damage to undead/dark enemies.
- Guardian ultimate now also grants 50% self damage reduction.
- Detailed final damage/healing/tanking statistics and balanced MVP scoring.
- Victory Dust pool 400–1000 total, split across participants.
- MVP pack chances: Base 40%, Premium 20%, Elite 15%, Boss 25%.
